Visualizing World Crop Production

by Christian Felt

This visualization, at the bottom of the page, helps one to see what crops are grown in the world, how much, when, and where. It shows production in dry tonnes from 1961 to 2014 for 173 crops in most countries. The data is from the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations.
